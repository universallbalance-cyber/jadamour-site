Refund Policy

General Policy

All sales are final once a submission has been accepted and distribution processing has begun. Because Jadamour Music immediately initiates the distribution process upon receiving payment, we are unable to offer refunds after work has started.

Exceptions

A full refund may be issued if:

  • Your submission was charged but never processed due to a technical error on our end.
  • You request a cancellation before distribution processing has begun (within 24 hours of payment).

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at jadamour_music@aol.com within 24 hours of your payment with your order ID and reason for the request. We will respond within 3 business days.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you use the Jadamour Music app, we collect information you provide directly, including your artist name, email address, social media handle, and the audio and artwork files you upload for distribution.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your music distribution submission.
  • To communicate with you about your order status.
  • To process payments securely through our payment processor (Stripe).

Payment Information

We do not store your credit card or payment details. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. Please review Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to fulfill your distribution order (e.g., submitting your music to streaming platforms) or as required by law.

Data Retention

We retain your submission data for the duration of your distribution agreement and as required for accounting and legal purposes.
